What is breast cancer and how does it occur?

Doctors know that breast cancer occurs when some breast cells begin to grow abnormally. These cells divide more rapidly than healthy cells do and continue to accumulate, forming a lump or mass. Cells may spread (metastasize) through your breast to your lymph nodes or to other parts of your body.

At what age does breast cancer symptoms start in female?

Most breast cancers are found in women who are 50 years old or older. Some women will get breast cancer even without any other risk factors that they know of. Having a risk factor does not mean you will get the disease, and not all risk factors have the same effect.

How can we understand we are having breast cancer

To understand if you might have breast cancer, watch for signs like lumps, changes in breast size or shape, skin abnormalities, and nipple changes. Consult a healthcare professional for a clinical examination and consider screening tests like mammograms or ultrasounds. A biopsy can confirm the presence of cancer. Early detection is important, so seek medical attention if you notice any concerning breast changes.
